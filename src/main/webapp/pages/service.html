Travel Assistance Services

When you purchase travel insurance, you expect support in case of unexpected events. Travelers can face a range of challenges while on a trip, from medical concerns like needing to find a doctor to non-medical issues such as replacing a lost passport. Our travel assistance services offer multilingual support 24/7, ensuring help is available year-round. Services include medical referrals, hospital admission assistance, emergency evacuation, and coordination for lost luggage or passports.
